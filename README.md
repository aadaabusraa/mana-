# Privacy Policy for Mana

**Last Updated: January 12, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Mana ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Password (encrypted and securely stored)
- Display name
- Profile photo (optional)

### 2.2 Profile Information
You may choose to provide:
- Phone number
- Date of birth
- Gender
- Bio/description
- Address (street, city, state, country, postal code)

### 2.3 Location Data
With your permission, we collect:
- Precise location (latitude and longitude)
- Approximate location
- Location address

Location data is used to connect you with nearby users and provide location-based services.

### 2.4 Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Help requests created and completed
- Help provided to other users
- User ratings and reviews
- Login timestamps
- App usage statistics

### 2.5 Device Permissions
Our app requests the following permissions:
- **Location**: To provide location-based services
- **Camera**: To upload profile photos or images
- **Storage**: To save and access photos and files

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Provide our services and features
- Connect you with other users for help requests
- Send notifications about your account and activity
- Improve our app and user experience
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage
Your data is stored securely using Firebase services provided by Google LLC. Data is stored on servers located in various regions and is encrypted in transit and at rest.

### 4.2 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

### 4.3 Data Retention
We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these situations:

### 5.1 With Other Users
- Your display name, profile photo, and public profile information are visible to other users
- Your location may be shared when using location-based features
- Your ratings and reviews are visible to other users

### 5.2 Service Providers
We share data with trusted third-party service providers:
- **Firebase/Google Cloud**: For authentication, database, and hosting services
- These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and only process data on our behalf

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Enforce our terms of service
- Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- Respond to emergency situations

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Control
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Update or correct your information
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of communications

### 6.2 Location Services
You can disable location services at any time through your device settings. This may limit certain app features.

### 6.3 Notifications
You can control notification preferences within the app settings or your device settings.

### 6.4 Camera and Storage
You can revoke camera and storage permissions through your device settings. This may prevent certain features from working.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

## 9. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Authentication**: For secure user authentication
- **Cloud Firestore**: For data storage
- **Firebase Analytics**: For app usage analytics

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## 10. Cookies and Tracking

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve our app. You can opt-out of analytics through your device settings or app settings.
